Now, Let's Think About This...

Look at your wheel shape. What jumps out at you?
Is it lopsided? Super balanced? Smaller than you expected? Just notice without judging.
Any surprises here?
Sometimes we don't realize how good (or not so good) certain areas are until we stop and actually think about it.
If you could only improve ONE area this month, which would it be?
And here's the kicker - what's one tiny thing you could do about it this week? Like, embarrassingly small. That's where we start.
What's actually working well?
Seriously, give yourself some credit here. What are you doing right that you could keep doing or even do more of?
Is there a connection between any of these areas?
Like, maybe when work is stressful, fun takes a hit? Or when relationships are good, everything else feels easier?
